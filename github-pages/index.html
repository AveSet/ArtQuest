Геймифицированное десктоп-приложение для регулярной практики рисования и анимации. Прогресс хранится локально; синхронизация галереи с Google Drive — только по вашему выбору.

О приложении

Русский

ArtQuest — десктопное приложение для Windows, которое помогает художникам и аниматорам выстраивать регулярную практику. Прогресс, настройки и статистика хранятся на вашем устройстве. Внешней аналитики и облачного бэкенда у приложения нет.

Возможности

  • Каталог творческих заданий (квестов) с уровнями сложности
  • Дерево навыков и прокачка по категориям
  • Ежедневная практика, стрики и достижения
  • Личная галерея работ
  • Материалы и референсы для обучения

Google Drive (опционально)

Если вы подключаете Google-аккаунт, ArtQuest может сохранять файлы галереи (изображения и видео ваших работ) в папку My Drive → ArtQuest → Gallery. Приложение использует OAuth и scope drive.file — доступ только к файлам, которые создаёт само приложение. Отключить синхронизацию можно в любой момент в настройках.

English

ArtQuest is a Windows desktop application that helps artists and animators build a consistent practice routine. Progress, settings, and statistics stay on your device. The app does not run external analytics or a cloud backend.

Features

  • Structured creative quests with difficulty levels
  • Skill tree with category progression
  • Daily practice, streaks, and achievements
  • Personal artwork gallery
  • Learning materials and references

Google Drive (optional)

When you connect Google Drive, ArtQuest can store gallery files in My Drive → ArtQuest → Gallery. The app uses OAuth with the drive.file scope and only accesses files it creates. You can disconnect at any time in Settings.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

ArtQuest ("we", "our", or "the app") is a desktop application for gamified drawing and animation practice. This Privacy Policy explains how ArtQuest handles information when you use the application, including the optional Google Drive sync feature.

2. Data stored locally

By default, ArtQuest stores all data on your device, including:

  • Quest progress, skill levels, and achievements
  • App settings and preferences
  • Gallery metadata and local gallery files
  • Practice statistics and session history

This data is not transmitted to our servers. ArtQuest does not operate a backend that collects user data.

3. Optional Google Drive sync

If you choose to connect your Google account, ArtQuest uses the Google Drive API with the drive.file scope to:

  • Create and access a folder at My Drive/ArtQuest/Gallery
  • Upload, download, and manage gallery image and video files you save through the app
  • Store file metadata (file name, checksum, gallery item ID) as Google Drive app properties

ArtQuest does not access other files in your Google Drive. You can disconnect Google Drive at any time in app settings.

4. Google account information

When you sign in with Google, ArtQuest receives OAuth tokens to access Google Drive on your behalf. We do not receive or store your Google password. OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device and are used only for Drive sync operations you initiate.

5. Data we do not collect

ArtQuest does not:

  • Collect personal information on external servers
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising trackers
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Access your Google Drive files outside the ArtQuest/Gallery folder created by the app

6. Third-party services

Google Drive sync is subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. External links in the app (e.g., YouTube, Pinterest for learning materials) open in your browser and are governed by those services' own policies.

7. Data security

Gallery files and progress data are stored on your local device. When Google Drive sync is enabled, files are transmitted to your Google account over encrypted connections (HTTPS). You are responsible for securing your device and Google account.

8. Data deletion

  • Local data: delete progress and gallery files in app settings or by uninstalling ArtQuest.
  • Google Drive: files in My Drive/ArtQuest/Gallery remain until you delete them. Disconnecting sync in the app does not remove uploaded files.

9. Children's privacy

ArtQuest is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The "Last updated" date reflects changes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy are answered on the Contact page.
